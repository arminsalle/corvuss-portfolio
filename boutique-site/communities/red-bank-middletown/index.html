Red Bank / Middletown

Immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere and rich history of Red Bank and Middletown, two dynamic communities nestled along the banks of the Navesink River in Monmouth County, New Jersey.

At Boutique Realty, we’re passionate about these vibrant towns and are dedicated to helping you find your ideal home in this thriving area.

Red Bank, on the southern banks of the Navesink River and around five miles from the Atlantic Ocean, offers history, diversity, culture, and entertainment for those seeking a hip, urban vibe. Along Red Bank’s main commercial thoroughfares are dozens of restaurants, cafes, shops, clubs, and galleries, as well as numerous banks and brokerage houses.
