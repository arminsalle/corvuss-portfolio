Holmdel / Colts Neck

Embrace the tranquility and luxury of Holmdel and Colts Neck, two picturesque towns nestled in Monmouth County, New Jersey, where rolling landscapes, top-rated schools, and a rich cultural scene create an unparalleled quality of life.

At Boutique Realty, we’re passionate about these communities and are dedicated to helping you find your perfect home in this coveted area.

With a combined population of over 30,000 residents, Holmdel and Colts Neck offer a blend of suburban charm and cosmopolitan convenience. Families are drawn to the area for its excellent schools, including Holmdel High School and Colts Neck High School, both renowned for their academic excellence and extracurricular opportunities.
