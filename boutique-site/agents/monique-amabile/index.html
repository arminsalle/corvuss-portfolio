Monique Amabile has been in working in executive sales for Fortune 500 companies for over 20 years. Her passion for real estate began in1985 while starting construction on her personal residence. In addition to buying/selling homes and apartment complexes for herself she was running her own company. Real estate then became her number one priority for she knew she was destined to make a difference.

Two decades of experience with impressive career sales, Monique possesses a repository of knowledge of what Jersey City has to offer for the range of individuals she serves. Building a network of trusted industry professionals, including mortgage brokers, home inspectors, real estate attorneys and insurance agents who work hard and make the process go smoothly from start to finish allowing her clients to be stress free.

Selling…Monique has a proven marketing strategy. Through a combination of advertising, online exposure and direct mail, she takes every opportunity to put your property in front of the right potential buyers. She is able to start promoting each listing as quickly as possible with a strong marketing support team.

Purchasing…First time buyers or veterans to the industry will know that their agent is not in front or behind but beside them during every step of their journey.

A born and raised native who will provide extensive experience in any Hudson County Community you select. Downtown Jersey City is the place to be for a carefree walk able lifestyle. Neighborhoods include: Newport, Exchange Place, Liberty Harbor, Paulus Hook, and Hamilton Park. Looking for areas in transition JC/Heights, Bergen Lafayette, McKinley and Journal Square, West Bergen, and County Village will enable you to find that deal you are seeking. If you wish to try a suburb in New Jersey Monique also works in Bergen, Essex, Passaic and Monmouth counties and has the knowledge to assist you through any real estate transaction.

The future of real estate is continued excellent customer service given by a seasoned professional who listens to your needs be it Buyer/Seller, Renter/Tenant or commercial endeavor and is with you during the entire process giving you what a cloud company cannot. Monique wishes to make “Her Jersey City Your Jersey City”
