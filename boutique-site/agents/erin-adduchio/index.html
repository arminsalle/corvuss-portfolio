I graduated from Ramapo College of New Jersey in 2004 with a Bachelor’s Degree in Communications, as well as my elementary education teaching license. Prior to getting started in real estate I worked an assistant for the New York City District Council of Carpenters, and later for Goldman Sachs. In 2013, I moved with my husband to Northern Virginia where I began my career in real estate working for a top producing agent in the area. After moving back to NJ, I finally pursued my dream and obtained my real estate license!

Communication is key, I keep clients fully informed throughout the home buying or selling process. If you’re not left with an amazing experience, I haven’t done my job. I don’t measure success through achievements or awards, but through my client’s satisfaction.
