Stacey Bonavitacola

Sales Associate
Phone201.927.5884Emailstacey@boutiquerealtynj.comOffice215 Newark Ave, Jersey City, NJ 07302

I graduated with a degree in Media Arts and continued my education at the graduate level at Saint Peter’s College. My first experience with real estate was in property management for The Rouse Company at the South Street Seaport in NYC. Soon after I started selling real estate throughout Hoboken and Jersey City. Originally I specialized in old world charm properties but then embraced the new construction boom, working for Fields Development Group. In 2010 my colleague & partner Melissa Pasquariello and I developed Boutique Realty Moms. The areas first real estate team to embrace the latest baby boom and specialize in making real estate transactions as stress free as possible for families. Together we have formed lasting relationships with many families and assisted them with making the move to Hoboken/JC and the surrounding Suburbs of Westfield, Scotch Plains, Fanwood, Montclair, Wayne to name a few. We also worked with developer, MJN Partners as the exclusive sales team of The Vestry in Hoboken.

For nearly the past decade I have enjoyed meeting new people and making their dreams a reality, whether it be buying an investment property or finding them their dream home.

I am pleased to say that many of these people have become loyal clients and have continued to buy, sell and lease property with me as well as trust me with the referral of a close friend or family member. My success is the result of enthusiasm, honesty and integrity. I believe that the two greatest tools in life are dedication and experience.
