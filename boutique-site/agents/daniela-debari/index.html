Daniela Debari

Phone551.655.8294Emaildd@pillaripropertiesgroup.comOffice215 Newark Ave, Jersey City, NJ 07302

Daniela De Bari is a Realtor Associate with three years of experience in the industry. She specializes in residential real estate and is committed to providing the highest levels of customer service throughout the entire real estate process.

Daniela has deep experience in the renovation and rehabilitation of residential homes, having worked with her family on a number of investment properties, and has developed a keen sense of design when breathing new life into a home and property. With her experience and her affiliation with Special Properties, Daniela is able to leverage the strength of Christie’s and Special Properties’ unparalleled corporate marketing resources and superior customer service to support her clients.

In her spare time, Daniela enjoys fitness, hiking, travel, and is an avid foodie. She is a graduate of Montclair State University with a bachelor’s degree in nutrition. Daniela is a lifelong resident of Hudson County, born and raised in the Heights section of Jersey City.
