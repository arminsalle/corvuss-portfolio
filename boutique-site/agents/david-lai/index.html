I’ve never been the type of person to just have one job, one thing to do, one place to be. I’m a serial multitasker and find ways to do more all the time. I like being a part of an industry that is people focused. Working with my clients is the best part of my job. Anytime I can advise, connect, and guide them to make the best decision for themselves on a purchase or a sale is why I do what I do.

Nothing is more rewarding than that. I try my best to give them the information they need to make the best decisions for themselves. Purchasing a property is a big commitment that comes with a range of emotions. As a social worker as well I definitely connect with my clients in a different way and I love the relationships that are created. Having the client close on their new home is a very fulfilling thing as is having a client sell their home to move on to the next chapter in their lives. The business of helping people is doing well and I hope to connect with many more new clients in the future.
