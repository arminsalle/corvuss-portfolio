Dana Berkowitz

Sales Associate
Phone914.707.4187Emaildana@boutiquerealtynj.comOffice215 Newark Ave, Jersey City, NJ 07302

After graduating from Binghamton University, majoring in English and French, I moved to Manhattan to work for a French textile house. I then spent a decade as Senior Design Consultant at Waterworks, designing bathrooms and kitchens in NYC’s most luxurious homes. With a knack for design and a passion for real estate, I began ‘flipping’ my own properties in NYC. This hobby of mine led me to buy and restore an old carriage house in Mérida, Mexico on the Yucatán peninsula, where I lived for 3 years. As fate would have it, I then married a contractor back in the States, who shares my love for real estate, architecture and design. Our first project was our own 160 year old Victorian in the heart of Red Bank, NJ. As the daughter of a real estate broker, it was only natural that my personal experience in real estate and my love for interior design would lead me to becoming a Realtor
