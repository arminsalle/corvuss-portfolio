Amy Udell

Sales Associate
Phone617.650.7575Emailamy@boutiquerealtynj.comOffice215 Newark Ave, Jersey City, NJ 07302

A longtime resident of Hoboken, Amy specializes in the Hoboken and Downtown Jersey City real estate market. Originally born and raised in the Boston area, Amy later moved to Manhattan and has since relocated to Hoboken. She understands the challenges that come with finding and re-establishing a home in a new city and makes the process of buying or selling as smooth as possible. Prior to joining Boutique, Amy oversaw and executed media campaigns for Fortune 500 companies at one of the largest advertising agencies in Manhattan. With over 8 years of professional marketing experience along with a B.A. in Advertising and Public Relations, Amy offers unparalleled expertise when it comes to marketing and selling properties. Her passion for the local community combined with her strong business acumen and hard-working attitude allows her to provide clients with an unsurpassed experience.
