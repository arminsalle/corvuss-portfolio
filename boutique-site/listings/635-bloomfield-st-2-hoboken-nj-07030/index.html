Welcome home to this bright and spacious two-bedroom residence on desirable, tree-lined Bloomfield Street. Located on the third floor, this freshly painted apartment features high ceilings, a large skylight that fills the living space with natural light, and charming exposed-brick walls. The thoughtfully designed layout offers two oversized bedrooms positioned on opposite sides of the apartment for added privacy. The open-concept kitchen, living and dining area is ideal for both everyday living and entertaining. The kitchen includes solid-wood cabinetry, granite countertops, stainless-steel appliances and a generous breakfast bar. Additional highlights include central air conditioning and heat, hardwood floors, recessed lighting, excellent natural light and the convenience of an in-unit washer and dryer. Ideally situated just one block from Washington Street and close to Hoboken's shops, restaurants, cafés, nightlife, parks, buses, PATH transportation and more.
