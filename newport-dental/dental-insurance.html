Dental Insurance.

Financing Your Care

At Newport Family and Cosmetic Dentistry and Portsmouth Dental Associates, we want your dental care to be as smooth and stress-free as possible. Dental insurance can be confusing, and coverage varies based on the plan your employer has selected. For details about your benefits, please review the insurance booklet provided by your employer.

We will give you an estimate of your insurance coverage before treatment based on the information received from your insurance company. Please note that this is only an estimate, not a guarantee of payment.

As a courtesy, we file all insurance claims for you. However, any balance not covered by your insurance remains your responsibility. If your insurance pays more or less than expected, we will adjust your account accordingly.

If you have questions about your coverage, our financial policies, or financing options, our team is always happy to help.
