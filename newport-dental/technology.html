How Advanced Technology Shapes Better Dental Care

Every tool in our office has a purpose: to enhance accuracy, safety, and comfort. Digital dentistry lets us diagnose earlier, plan treatments more precisely, and complete procedures with minimal disruption to your day. We’re not just using gadgets—we’re using them to improve your experience from start to finish.

CBCT Imaging and Digital X-rays

Traditional X-rays gave us a snapshot; CBCT and digital radiography give us a full story. CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) provides 3D images of your teeth, jaw, and bone structure with remarkable clarity. We can analyze even the tiniest details—vital for dental implants, root canals, and complex cases. Digital X-rays reduce radiation exposure while delivering instant results that help us explain your treatment plan clearly.

Intraoral Cameras and Scanning

Transparency matters. With our intraoral cameras and high-resolution scanners, you can see exactly what we see. Instead of guessing or relying on abstract descriptions, you’ll view detailed images of your teeth and gums on-screen. These tools also make digital impressions smoother, eliminating the need for uncomfortable trays and molds.

Technology in Newport Restorations: Precision Meets Comfort

CEREC technology allows us to craft crowns, veneers, and onlays right here in the office—often in a single visit. No waiting for lab work, no temporary crowns, no follow-up hassles. Using digital scanning and 3D printing, we design your restoration to blend perfectly with your natural teeth.

3D printing isn’t just for restorations—it also helps us produce precise surgical guides for implants and orthodontic models that make treatment more predictable and efficient. Every design comes from a digital blueprint customized for your smile.

Enhancing Hygiene and Comfort

Preventive care thrives on precision, too. With Guided Biofilm Therapy (GBT), we use airflow and gentle powders to remove plaque and bacteria without the discomfort of scraping. It’s ideal for sensitive patients and those with dental implants. You’ll leave with cleaner teeth and healthier gums—without the post-appointment soreness.

We also integrate Oraverse, a local anesthesia reversal agent, to help you regain normal sensation faster after treatment. It’s a small touch that makes a big difference, especially when you have a busy day ahead.

Digital Coordination for Seamless Care

Our use of Electronic Health Records ensures your dental history, imaging, and treatment plans stay accurate and easily accessible. This integration allows us to coordinate care between our two locations—Newport Family & Cosmetic Dentistry in Newport and Portsmouth Dental Associates in Portsmouth—with complete consistency. Whether you’re here for a routine checkup or a cosmetic enhancement, every detail is securely documented and available for your comfort and safety.
