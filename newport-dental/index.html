Technology

Every tool has
a purpose.

To enhance accuracy, safety, and comfort. Digital dentistry lets us diagnose earlier, plan treatments more precisely, and complete procedures with minimal disruption to your day.

Explore our technology
  • CBCT 3D ImagingFull 3D images of teeth, jaw, and bone — vital for implants, root canals, and complex cases.
  • CEREC Same-Day RestorationsCrowns, veneers, and onlays crafted in-office — no temporaries, no second visit.
  • Intraoral Cameras & ScanningSee exactly what we see. Digital impressions with no uncomfortable trays or molds.
  • 3D PrintingPrecise surgical guides for implants and orthodontic models, custom to your smile.
  • Guided Biofilm TherapyAirflow cleaning that removes plaque without scraping — ideal for sensitive patients.
  • Digital X-Rays & OraverseLess radiation, instant results — and faster return of normal sensation after treatment.
