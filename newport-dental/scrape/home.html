Welcome

blending modern dentistry with a warm environment

Newport, RI, Dentists Nathan W. Tilman, DDS, Bryan May, DDS, Dianne Pannes, DDS and James Safford, DDS, practice a full scope of general and cosmetic dentistry with expertise ranging from porcelain veneers to dental implants, crowns and bridges. Dr. Tilman, Dr. May, Dr. Pannes, and Dr. Safford can now correct a wide variety of so-called permanent cosmetic dental problems and can literally redesign your smile.

Why Choose Us

Two Locations

With offices in both Newport and Portsmouth, we make it easy to get the care you need close to home. Each location offers the same friendly team, advanced technology, and personalized service—so you can enjoy a seamless experience wherever you visit.

Advanced Technology

Our practice combines innovation with precision through the use of advanced tools like CBCT scanning, digital impressions, iTero scanners, and CEREC same-day restorations. With the help of OverJet AI, 3D printing, and detailed digital imaging, we deliver faster, more accurate, and more comfortable care.

Comforts & Amenities

We believe your dental visit should be as relaxing as possible, which is why we offer a variety of thoughtful amenities. Enjoy hot beverages, Wi-Fi, TVs, and streaming services—all in a comfortable, welcoming environment with same-day appointments available.

Two Locations

Visit our Newport or Portsmouth Dental Offices

Newport Family & Cosmetic Dentistry proudly serves patients across Aquidneck Island with two convenient locations in Newport and Portsmouth. No matter which office you visit, you’ll experience the same warm, personalized care and advanced dentistry our practice is known for. Both locations are equipped with modern technology, a welcoming atmosphere, and a team dedicated to making every visit comfortable and stress-free. We invite you to choose the office that’s most convenient for you—and discover a dental experience centered around your comfort, health, and smile.
