Vanson Leathers - Motorcycle Jackets Hand Made in the USA

Each Vanson is designed, engineered, cut and hand made in the U.S.A. of imported and domestic materials.
(Note: We do carry a few items that are imported which are noted on their corresponding web page).
Vanson is a leading manufacturer of high quality handcrafted American leather motorcycle jackets. You can choose from; custom men's and women's leather jackets, off-the-rack leather motorcycle jackets and suits, custom racing suits, Cafe Racer Jackets, Concealed Carry Jackets, and other performance motorcycle products.

What makes a Vanson a Vanson?

Vanson has one of the largest selections of American made leather jackets, pants, vests and for those not wanting leather, textile motorcycle apparel - The selection covers leather jackets for motorcycling, road racing, drag racing, cruising/touring, casual street, as well as urban fashion (Metro) for everyday wear, all handcrafted by Vanson Leathers, that is what it is all about. Add to that, Vanson Leathers Limited Warranty, it is valid for one year from the date of purchase on all our leather garments against defects in workmanship and materials.

Racing Leather 

Custom leather motorcycle racing suits is the DNA of Vanson. Vanson has been in business since 1974; we are a small operation, we specialize, and everything we do is done very well. Buy a suit from us, and you may be paying more up-front (we reserve the right to be cheap), but in the long run it's likely to cost you less, and for sure will serve you better along the way.

We don't just stitch these things together - we design them. Vanson has 10 patents in ventilation and protection. What other racing suit company can say that?
